Trust Your Data.
From the Very First Byte.

Xafron provides intelligent data validation and anomaly detection at the source, ensuring every pipeline is built on a foundation of clean, reliable data.

The Problem

Garbage In, Garbage Everywhere

Data quality is often an afterthought. Errors originating at the source silently corrupt your entire data pipeline, leading to flawed analytics, broken models, and costly, preventable mistakes.

Neglected at the Source

Validation is typically applied too late, after bad data has already spread.

Propagating Errors

A single source error can cause a cascade of failures downstream.

Unintended Consequences

Results in unreliable reports, poor business decisions, and loss of trust in data.

The Solution

Validate and Purify at the Point of Entry

Xafron integrates directly with your data sources, providing a powerful suite of tools to detect anomalies, validate data, and perform automatic cleanups before bad data ever enters your system.

Source-Level Anomaly Detection

Identify unexpected patterns, outliers, and deviations in real-time as data is generated.

Customizable Error Validation

Define and enforce bespoke validation rules tailored to your specific business logic and data types.

Automatic Cleanup & Feedback

Automate data correction for common issues and provide direct feedback to data providers via an intuitive dashboard.

Our Technology

Intelligently Engineered for Accuracy

We leverage a sophisticated blend of Large Language Models and proven machine learning techniques to deliver robust, nuanced, and self-improving data validation.

LLM-Based Learning

We use LLMs to interpret data and generate human-readable feedback, making error resolution faster and more intuitive.

Validators & Evaluators

Our system uses precise error functions calculated from custom validators and evaluators to create highly reliable field validation.

Auto-Encoders

A key part of our anomaly detection, auto-encoders learn the "normal" state of your data to flag deviations.

Case-Based Reasoning (CBR)

By comparing new data points to a library of historical cases, CBR helps identify subtle and context-specific anomalies.

Embedding-Based Similarity

We transform data into vector embeddings to perform powerful similarity searches, uncovering inconsistencies that other methods miss.

Scalable Architecture

Built for performance and reliability, our architecture can handle high-volume data streams without compromising speed.
